Command

du -sh --max-depth=1 . | sort -rh
`du`: disk usage - `-sh`: summerize the information in human readable form - `--max-depth=1`: show information in less than or equal to 1 depth - `.`: current folder is the target folder - `|`: pipe command - `sort -rh`: sort the information in reverse order (biggest to smallest) and in human readable form
